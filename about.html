About FitMetrics

Our Mission

FitMetrics was created to provide simple and reliable health calculators that anyone can use for free. Understanding health metrics such as BMI, calorie expenditure, hydration and macronutrients can help individuals make better lifestyle decisions.

Many online tools hide useful calculations behind complex apps or subscriptions. Our goal is to provide straightforward tools that are accessible directly through your browser.

What We Provide

Our website offers a growing collection of tools including:

  • BMI Calculator – estimate body mass index.
  • TDEE Calculator – estimate daily calorie expenditure.
  • Macro Calculator – determine macronutrient targets.
  • Body Fat Calculator – estimate body fat percentage.
  • Water Intake Calculator – estimate hydration needs.

These calculators are based on commonly accepted formulas used in nutrition science and sports medicine.

Educational Resources

In addition to calculators, we also provide educational guides explaining the science behind nutrition, hydration and body composition. Our aim is to make health knowledge easier to understand.

Medical Disclaimer
