About FitMetrics

Building tools that make health science accessible to everyone — free, forever.

Our Mission

FitMetrics was built on a simple idea: everyone deserves access to accurate, science-based health tools without needing to download an app, create an account, or pay a subscription. Understanding your body — your BMI, daily calorie needs, macronutrient targets, hydration levels, and body composition — should not be locked behind a paywall or buried in complexity.

We believe that when people have clear, reliable information about their health metrics, they make better decisions. Better decisions lead to better habits. Better habits lead to better lives. That is the chain of value we are trying to create with every calculator and guide we publish.

The Science Behind Our Tools

Every calculator uses validated formulas from peer-reviewed research. Our BMI and TDEE tools use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the most accurate formula for estimating metabolic rate in the general population. Body fat uses the US Army circumference method. Heart rate zones use the Karvonen formula. Ideal weight uses the Devine formula.

Where results involve estimation rather than measurement, we communicate this clearly and recommend consulting a healthcare professional for clinical assessment.
