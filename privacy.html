Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — clearly explained.

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

At FitMetrics ("we", "our", "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit quickbmi.in, how we use it, and your rights. By using our website you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Calculator inputs

All health calculations are performed entirely within your browser. Values you enter — weight, height, age, body measurements — are never transmitted to our servers, never stored, and never shared. They exist only in your browser's memory during your session and are discarded when you leave the page.

Automatically collected information

Like most websites, we automatically collect technical information including anonymised IP address, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited, time and date of visit, and referring URL. This is collected through Google Analytics and used solely to understand how visitors use our site.

Cookies

We use cookies for three purposes:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) — understand traffic patterns. Do not personally identify you.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense, if active) — serve relevant advertisements based on browsing behaviour.
  • Preference cookies — may store basic display preferences.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to understand and improve our site, serve advertisements through Google AdSense, diagnose technical problems, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised usage data. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing history. Manage your preferences at myaccount.google.com or opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please read their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data, and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, use our contact form.

Data Retention

We do not store calculator inputs — there is no health data to retain. Anonymised analytics data is retained by Google Analytics for 14 months. Contact information is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Use our contact form.
