Frequently Asked Questions

Are all calculators free?

Yes — every calculator is completely free, forever. No sign-up, no subscription, no hidden fees.

Is my health data stored?

No. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We never store, transmit or have access to any health data you enter.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Absolutely. Select "Suggest a Calculator" in the subject dropdown. We review all suggestions and prioritise the most requested tools.

I found an error. What should I do?

Select "Report a Bug" and include the calculator name and the result you expected. We fix confirmed bugs as quickly as possible.

Can I use your calculators on my website?

Please contact us with a "Business Enquiry" for licensing or partnership questions.
