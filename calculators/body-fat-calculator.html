Why Body Fat % Matters More Than Weight

Body fat percentage is a more meaningful indicator of health than weight or BMI alone because it tells you the actual proportion of fat versus lean tissue in your body. Two people can weigh the same but have very different health profiles depending on their body composition. Read our full guide: Healthy Body Fat Percentage Explained.

FAQs

How accurate is this method?

This waist-to-height method provides an estimate within approximately 3–5%. For greater accuracy, consider a DEXA scan or skinfold caliper test by a professional.

What is a healthy body fat percentage?

For men: 14–24% is acceptable to fitness range. For women: 21–31% is acceptable to fitness range. Athletes typically fall below these ranges.

Can I reduce body fat without losing weight?

Yes — body recomposition involves gaining muscle while losing fat simultaneously. This is most effective for beginners and people returning to training after a break.
