Why Body Fat Percentage Matters

Body fat percentage measures the proportion of fat compared to the rest of your body mass. Unlike weight alone, it provides a clearer understanding of physical fitness and overall health.

Someone may weigh more due to muscle mass but still have a healthy body fat level. This is why body fat percentage is often a more meaningful measurement than weight alone.

FAQs

What is a healthy body fat percentage?

Healthy ranges depend on gender and age, but generally men fall between 10–20% and women between 18–28%.

Is this calculator perfectly accurate?

This tool provides an estimate. Medical tests such as DEXA scans or hydrostatic weighing are more precise.

Can exercise reduce body fat?

Yes. Regular exercise combined with a calorie-controlled diet can help lower body fat percentage over time.
