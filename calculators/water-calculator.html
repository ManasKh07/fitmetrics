The Importance of Daily Hydration

Water plays a crucial role in nearly every biological process in the human body. It helps regulate body temperature, transport nutrients, remove waste products and support cellular function.

Many health professionals recommend roughly 30–35 milliliters of water per kilogram of body weight daily. This calculator uses a baseline of 33 ml/kg to estimate hydration needs.

Hydration works best alongside proper nutrition. Also calculate your daily macro intake or estimate daily calorie requirements. Learn more in our Daily Water Intake Guide.

FAQs

Does coffee count toward hydration?

Yes, beverages like coffee and tea contribute to daily fluid intake, although water should remain the primary source.

How can I tell if I'm dehydrated?

Dark yellow urine, fatigue and headaches are common signs of dehydration.

Should athletes drink more water?

Yes. Physical activity and sweating increase fluid loss and therefore increase hydration requirements.
